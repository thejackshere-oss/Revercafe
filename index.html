About Rever Cafe

Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional coffee and timeless design, Rever Cafe represents a fusion of modern sensibilities with heritage aesthetics. We believe that a great café is more than just a place to grab coffee—it's a sanctuary where connections are made and moments are cherished.

Our glasshouse setting echoes Kolkata's architectural heritage while our approach to coffee and cuisine embraces contemporary excellence. Every element, from the furniture to the menu, has been carefully curated to create an experience that feels both timeless and current.

We're committed to sustainability, quality sourcing, and supporting our local community. Come experience the Rever difference.
